WHY DID
YOU CHOOSE
TVL-ICT?

Clark Wayne A. Abutal

TVL-ICT 11

Being able to be a part of TVL-ICT 11, was an
astonishing achievement for me. TVL-ICT is
one of the track/strand of Fidelis Senior High.
It focuses and promotes the use and study
of technology. For as long as I can remember,
I have dreamt of becoming a programmer.
At a young age, I was introduced at computers
that helped me in deciding for my future.
Technology always amuses me. Where others see
the technicalities, and programming as workloads,
I only see excitement. And choosing
this track was the right decision,
for it produces the best out of me.
